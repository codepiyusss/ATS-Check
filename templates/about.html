About This Project

Purpose

Check Your Resume was built to help job seekers get a quick, practical idea of how well their resume might perform against an Applicant Tracking System (ATS) before they submit it to a real job application.

Who It's For

Students, recent graduates, and anyone actively job hunting who wants fast feedback on formatting, keywords, and overall resume quality without needing to hire a professional resume reviewer.

How ATS Works

Most companies use software called an Applicant Tracking System to automatically scan and filter incoming resumes before a human ever sees them. These systems look for relevant keywords, clear formatting, and readable structure. A resume that isn't ATS-friendly can be filtered out even if the candidate is qualified.

This project estimates ATS compatibility using resume parsing, keyword analysis, formatting evaluation, and AI-generated feedback.
